In this digital artwork, a young woman is the central figure, standing under an umbrella in the rain. She is dressed in a black leather jacket and a red scarf, adding a pop of color to her outfit. Her blonde hair is wet from the rain, and she gazes directly at the viewer with a serious expression on her face. The background is blurred, suggesting a cityscape with streetlights and buildings. The artist's signature, "Wolfs", is visible in the bottom right corner of the image. The overall mood of the piece is somber and introspective.
